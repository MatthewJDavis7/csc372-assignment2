Travelogue: Kyoto, Japan

Overview

Kyoto is a city with a rich history and breathtaking natural beauty. It was once the imperial capital of Japan. As such, it offers a fascinating look at Japan's heritage.

Must-See Spots

  1. Nijo Castle
  2. Gion District
  3. Kinkaku-ji
  4. Kiyomizu-dera Temple
  5. Yasaka Shrine

Visitor Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes, there will be a lot of walking around.
  • Visiting tourist spots in the morning to avoid large crowds.
  • Make sure to study up on practical Japanese phrases.
  • Study up on Japanese traditions so you do not end up being disrespectful.
